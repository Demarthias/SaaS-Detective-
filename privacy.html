Privacy Policy

Effective Date: February 2, 2026
Entity: Venom Industries LLC

Zero-Data Promise: SaaS Detective operates on a "Local-First" architecture. We do not maintain servers to store your browsing history, passwords, or personal data.

1. Data Collection & Usage

We comply with the Chrome Web Store User Data Policy, including the "Limited Use" requirements.

  • Personal Data: We do NOT collect names, emails, addresses, or phone numbers.
  • Browsing Data: We do NOT track, log, or transmit your browsing history. The extension reads the activeTab DOM only upon your explicit request to identify technologies.
  • Technical Data: All technology detection (e.g., identifying "Shopify" or "React") happens locally on your device using bundled signatures.

2. Affiliate Disclosure (Strict Compliance)

SaaS Detective is supported by affiliate partnerships. To ensure compliance with Google's "Direct Benefit" policy (2025), we adhere to the following:

  • No Injection: We do not automatically inject or replace links in your content.
  • User Action Required: Affiliate links are only generated when you actively click a "View Deal" or "More Info" button within our UI.
  • Transparency: Clicking a partner link (e.g., to Shopify or MaxBounty) redirects you through an affiliate tracking URL. At this point, the third-party network may collect your IP address to attribute the referral.

3. Permissions Justification

We use the minimum permissions necessary:

  • activeTab: Grants temporary access to the current site to perform the scan.
  • storage: Saves your UI preferences (e.g., "Dark Mode") locally.
  • scripting: Allows the execution of the detection engine within the page context.

4. European Union Users (GDPR)

Since we do not collect personal data (PII), we act as a local tool provider rather than a Data Processor. No user data is transferred to US servers by Venom Industries LLC.
