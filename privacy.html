Privacy Policy - SaaS Detective

Last Updated: January 25, 2026

Developer: Venom Industries

Contact: gkube16@protonmail.com

1. Overview

SaaS Detective is a browser extension that helps users identify the technology stack and SaaS tools used on websites. This privacy policy explains how the extension operates and what data handling practices we follow.

2. Data Collection & Processing

What We Scan: The extension analyzes script tags and resource URLs loaded on the websites you visit.

What We Store: No data is stored. The extension operates entirely on your device in real-time.

What We Send: No data is sent to external servers. All processing is done locally in your browser.

No User Tracking: We do not track your browsing history, personal information, or install analytics.

3. Permissions Explained

  • activeTab - Required to access the current website you're viewing
  • scripting - Required to inject our detection script into web pages
  • https://*/* and http://*/* - Allows the extension to run on all websites (necessary for universal detection)

These permissions are used only for identifying tech stack signatures. No personal data is collected.

4. Affiliate Links Disclosure

SaaS Detective may include affiliate links to services detected on websites. When you click "Visit" on a detected tool, you may be directed through an affiliate link. This helps support the extension's development.

Transparency: We clearly label all visit buttons. You are never required to use these links.

No Cookie Injection: We do not inject tracking cookies or modify your browsing experience beyond providing the detection interface.

5. Third-Party Services

When you click affiliate links, you are directed to third-party websites. Please review their privacy policies separately, as we are not responsible for their data practices.

6. Data Security

Since we do not collect, store, or transmit any personal data, there is minimal security risk associated with using this extension.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy at any time. Updates will be reflected with an updated "Last Updated" date at the top of this page.

8. Questions or Concerns

If you have any questions about this privacy policy or how the extension operates, please contact us at gkube16@protonmail.com.
